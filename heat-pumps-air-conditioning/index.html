What type of air conditioning is most efficient?

Generally, central air conditioning is most efficient. If you’re looking for whole-house cooling, and you already have ductwork in your home, this is the most popular option. You can expect lower energy bills, more indoor comfort, cooler temperatures and lower humidity. However, if youre looking for something ductless or different, we have plenty of highly efficient options to suit your unique space.

Heat Pumps in Victoria, Services to Heat and Cool Your Home

With Vancouver Islands chilly winters and warm summers, heat pumps in Victoria are a great, high-efficiency way to heat and cool your home. Unlike a combustion heating unit like a furnace, heat pumps utilize the same technology found in fridges. They are an excellent energy-efficient heat source for your home, with the added benefit of cooling your home in the summer. Simply put, a heat pump will pull warm air from the outside and circulate it in your home during the winter months. In the summer, the process reverses, and the heat pump pulls the hot air from your house to be dispersed outside.
We have the experience to install, repair, or maintain your heat pump in Victoria.

What Are The Benefits of Heat Pumps?

The benefits of a heat pump include:

 

Theyre typically safer than combustion systems

Cheaper to run compared to gas and oil boilers

Reduces carbon emissions, has a very efficient conversion rate of energy to heat

Less maintenance than combustion system

Cools your home during the summer

Very long life-span, a reliable and steady heat source

How much does it cost for a heat pump system?

A heat pump costs on average, from about $6,000 to $14,000. The cost of a heat pump can vary depending on the type, model and installation needed to support your home’s characteristics and size.

How many years does a heat pump last?

A heat pump can last anywhere from 10-20 years if you’ve been maintaining your heating system. At around the 15-year mark, you may start to see a less efficient system.

Do heat pumps use a lot of electricity?

Heat pumps do not use a lot of electricity. Heat pumps are a very cost-effective alternative to traditional electric heating options because they move heat, rather than generate heat.

Dont Have Ductwork in Your Home? Not a Problem

A ductless heat pump system is an ideal way to heat and cool your home. They are an easy and customizable installation option that can accommodate most houses, townhouses, manufactured homes. Ductless heat pumps do not have ductwork, which eliminates concerns about dirty air ducts and improves indoor air quality. With significant savings over electric heat, ductless heat pumps can greatly reduce your energy bill. 4 Seasons chooses high-quality heat pumps from Daikin to install in your home.

What usually fails on a heat pump?

Failures that can contribute to your heat pump not cooling include low refrigerant levels, dirty air filters or coils, or a faulty thermostat. Physical blockages such as leaves and debris can get stuck in the system externally as well and prevent it from functioning properly.

Do heat pumps require annual maintenance?

It is recommended to annually maintain your heat pump with a professional technician. Moreover, if your heat pump is the primary heating and cooling system in your home rather than a supplementary system, more than once a year is suggested.

Can cold weather damage a heat pump?

Cold weather can technically damage a heat pump, if you have a heat pump not suited to your climate. Technological advances have allowed for the production of heat pumps specifically designed to withstand cold climates. Make sure you opt for the heat pump that suits your climate.
